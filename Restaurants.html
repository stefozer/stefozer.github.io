My Favorite Madison Restaurants!

Rare Steakhouse

  • The food at Rare, along with it's beautiful atmosphere make it a top notch restaurant
  • My favorite dishes are the mac and cheese, the tuna poke, the filet, and the hashbrowns
  • https://www.raresteaks.com

    Bar Taco

    • Bar taco is one of the best Mexican restaurants in all of Madison.
    • This is the type of restaurant to come with a group and get a lot of shareable's
    • My go-to order here is tuna poke, chips and guacamole, Mexican street corn, chicken taco, steak taco, cauliflower taco, and churros for dessert!
    • https://bartaco.com

      Ian's Pizza

      • Ian's Pizza is a madison staple
      • Ian's has a huge assortment of different flavor slices which are all so good
      • My personal favorites are the plain, mac and cheese, and drunken ravioli
      • The chopped salads are also amazing
      • https://ianspizza.com
